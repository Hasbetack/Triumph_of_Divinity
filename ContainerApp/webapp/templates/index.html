HOW A GAME IS WON

Victory Points

The player who has the most Victory Points at the end of the game is the winner. Victory Points can be obtained by completing Primary and Secondary Objectives. If both players have an equal number of Victory Points at the end of the game, it is considered a draw.

Primary Objectives

All factions have access to the following Primary Objective.

Raise the Banners

At the end of the Movement Step, any INFANTRY formation within 3” of an Objective Marker without a banner may raise a banner on that Objective. Make a Strength Check for that formation, and if it passes, that Objective is considered to have a friendly banner. Gain 1 Victory Point at the end of each round for each friendly banner.

If an INFANTRY formation is within 3” of an Objective Marker with an opponent’s banner, you may make a Strength Check for that formation, and if it passes, the opponent’s banner is destroyed.

Formations that performed a Dash action during the Movement Step or are Locked in Combat cannot attempt to raise or destroy Banners.

Formations that raised or lowered a banner cannot Charge in the same round.

Faction Specific Primaries

Some factions have a faction specific Primary Objective. These are available with that faction’s Datacards. The Raise the Banners Primary Objective must be replaced with a faction specific Primary Objective should one be available.
